About Skin Cancer

Skin cancer is the most common type of cancer globally. It occurs when the skin cells are damaged, usually by the sun's harmful ultraviolet (UV) rays. Skin cancer is highly treatable if detected early, making regular screenings and self-examinations crucial.

The Importance of Early Detection

Early detection of skin cancer significantly increases the chances of successful treatment. Regular screenings and self-examinations can help identify suspicious moles or lesions, prompting timely medical attention. Remember to protect your skin from excessive sun exposure and consult a healthcare professional if you notice any changes in your skin.
